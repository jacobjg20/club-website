Frequently Asked Questions

What time does our club meet?

We tend to meet at 6:00 pm on Monday evenings. The meetings usually run until around 8:30.

How often do you guys meet?

Once a week! (Monday evenings)

What’s so special about this “programming club”?

Ah. We’re glad you asked! We’re just a fun loving group of people who like to write code and get jiggy with it.

Do you guys teach formal lessons?

Not really. We’ll gladly help with any questions you may have, and if a bunch of you seem to have the same question, we’ll try to break it down for you on the board.
Otherwise, we’re mostly a group of nerds getting together to write some code for fun!

Ok Ok, but WHERE do you guys meet?

TE111 on the Gibbs campus!
